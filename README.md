# Cameron International (cameron-international)

Cameron International (formerly Cooper Cameron Corporation) was a leading provider of flow equipment products, systems, and services to the worldwide oil, gas, and process industries. In 2016 the business was acquired by Schlumberger (now SLB) and its pressure-control, drilling, subsea, valves, measurement, and compression product lines were integrated into SLB's digital oilfield portfolio. Cameron-branded capabilities are now delivered and integrated through the SLB DELFI digital platform and its developer APIs rather than through a standalone Cameron developer program.

## APIs

Cameron International no longer publishes standalone developer APIs under its own brand. Programmatic access to Cameron products, data, and workflows is offered through the SLB DELFI digital platform at [developer.delfi.slb.com](https://developer.delfi.slb.com/), which exposes a catalog of RESTful APIs for oil, gas, drilling, production, and subsea data covering the former Cameron equipment lines.
